## üö® STORYTELLING PROBLEMS

### Problem 1: Technical Details Break the Narrative
**Issue:** Slides 7-8 (Prompt/Context Engineering) are highly technical and come AFTER business value (Use Cases) but BEFORE the threat (Espionage).

**Current Arc:**
```
Why ‚Üí What ‚Üí How (Tech) ‚Üí Value ‚Üí How (More Tech) ‚Üí Threat ‚Üí Protection ‚Üí Action
```

**Problem:**
- Managers see business value (Use Cases)
- Then get hit with technical details (Prompt/Context)
- Then see threat (Espionage)
- **This breaks momentum and confuses the story**

**Better Arc:**
```
Why ‚Üí What ‚Üí Value ‚Üí Threat ‚Üí Protection ‚Üí Action
```

### Problem 2: Missing Investment & ROI
**Issue:** The Investment & ROI Framework slide (05c) is NOT in the current flow, but it's critical for managers.

**Impact:**
- Managers see Use Cases (value) but no cost/ROI context
- They can't make decisions without budget clarity
- German managers especially need this before technical details

### Problem 3: Threat Comes Too Late
**Issue:** The AI Espionage threat (slide 10) comes AFTER technical details.

**Problem:**
- Technical details (Prompt/Context) don't create urgency
- Threat creates urgency, but comes too late
- Managers may tune out during technical sections

**Better:** Threat should come right after Use Cases to create urgency before diving into governance.

## ‚úÖ RECOMMENDED FLOW (Storytelling Arc)

### The Story Arc Should Be:

1. **HOOK (Why Now)** - Create urgency
   - AI Momentum + Competitive Landscape
   - "We're behind, we need to act"

2. **WHAT (Core Concepts)** - Build understanding
   - AI Agents (what they are)
   - Technology Stack (what we're building)
   - MCP (how they connect)
   - Agentic AI (how they work together)

3. **VALUE (Business Impact)** - Show benefits
   - Use Cases (concrete examples)
   - Investment & ROI (costs and returns)
   - "Here's what you get and what it costs"

4. **THREAT (Urgency)** - Create urgency
   - AI Espionage Incident
   - "This is real, this is happening now"
   - "We must act, but we must act safely"

5. **PROTECTION (How We Stay Safe)** - Address concerns
   - Governance & Compliance
   - Security Risks & Mitigation
   - HITL (Human oversight)
   - Prompt/Context Engineering (how we ensure quality)
   - "Here's how we protect ourselves"

6. **ACTION (Path Forward)** - Clear next steps
   - AIOps (positive use case)
   - Change Management (organizational impact)
   - Failure Scenarios (contingency)
   - Roadmap (phased approach)
   - Closing (specific asks)
